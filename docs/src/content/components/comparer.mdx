Compare visual differences between similar content with a sliding panel.

For best results, use images that share the same dimensions. The slider can be controlled by dragging or pressing the <kbd aria-label="Left">←</kbd> and <kbd aria-label="Right">→</kbd> arrow keys. **Tip:** Press <kbd>Shift</kbd> + Arrow keys to move the slider in intervals of 10, or <kbd>Home</kbd> + <kbd>End</kbd> to jump to the beginning or the end.
